Emily Nagoski

@enagoski
ScienceSelf Help

  • Emily Nagoski, Ph.D. is a sex educator, researcher, and author with a doctorate in Health Behavior and a master's in Counseling from Indiana University.

  • She has taught human sexuality, relationships, and stress management at both graduate and undergraduate levels.

  • Nagoski's work focuses on empowering women to embrace their bodies and sexuality with confidence and joy.

  • She believes this goal, while seemingly small, has the power to change lives and ultimately impact the world.

  • Nagoski maintains an online presence through various social media platforms and her blog, where she continues to share her expertise and insights on human sexuality and well-being.
